Hey there, I'm Dekota, a full stack software engineer, err soon to be at least.

One day I was trying to look up the follow along materials for a class that, at the time, was a month old. I soon releazed just how difficult find the follow along info for a specific class was when it wasn't a recent class. I knew that people trying to catch up would face this same struggle and I wanted to help them. So, I made this site because I love building things and helping others.
